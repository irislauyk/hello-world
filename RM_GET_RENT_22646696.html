Full description

A mid terrace property which is to be let unfurnished includes carpets, gas central heating and UPVC double glazing.

To the ground floor there is lounge, separate dining room and fitted kitchen including integrated oven and hob.

From the landing there are two bedrooms and bathroom with three piece suite.

Outside, there is as garden area to the rear.
